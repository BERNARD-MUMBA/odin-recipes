INSHIMA

Inshima or Ubwali is Zambias staple food liked so much by many of us in Zambia. It is eaten with different types of relish. Among the favourite relis is Chicken, Porck, Bief, Beans, kapenta and many more. In central province of Zambia and Lamba land of copperbelt it is called Inshima. Wile in Bemba speaking lands, including Lusaka it is called Ubwali

In Zambia Inshima (Ubwali)) is is regarded as a very speciial meal, which when a Zambin hasn't eaten it will say hasn't eaten, even if has taken other foods. When prepared with Chicken it is regarded as a very special meal taken at special ocasions and used in most cases to welcome visitors. Traditionally Inshima is taken with kapenta, Chibwabwa, Ichinyeka(Chikanda) , Inswa, Inshonkono to mention but a feel.
