How to make chikanda or ichinyeka

Chikanda is one of the most liked recipes in Zambia. in my mother tongue LALA, the languege spocken in the central province of Zambia it is called ICHINYEKA. In big cities of Zambia like Lusaka and Kitwe it is mostly taken as a snack just like we take bollon, nevertheless ohers take it with inshima (Zambias staple food)

wether you take it with inshima or as a snack, it is such a iresistible recipe. More especially when you spice it up with Onion,tomatoe source and chili. Others still like it with flied eggs spiced with green paper, tomatoe, taking it with inshima.

Ingredients

  • powdered chikanda
  • powdered ground nuts
  • salt
  • soda
  • chili (option)
  • water
  • soda desolved in water less than 50 mills
